My work covers wireframing, visual design, art direction, UI design, motion and interactive prototypes. This approach enables me to create thoughtful experiences by focusing on the brand's character and intent. Overall, I love to pay attention to detail and care for design execution and emphasizing collaboration with stakeholders throughout all project phases.

I enjoy working with enthusiastic people so, within the last couple of years, I've been working with multiple design teams, in person and remotely, but also I've been fortunate to work and collaborate with some talented freelances, studios and agencies, crafting meaningful and remarkable digital experiences for various brands.

My work has been recognized with various awards and features across different platforms, reinforcing my commitment to pushing creative boundaries and delivering work that resonates with audiences worldwide.

Background

I'm Leonard Semmler, a designer based in Hamburg, Germany with a B.Sc. in Human-Computer Interaction. My work lives at the intersection of strategy and aesthetics — I believe the best design doesn't just look good, it communicates clearly and moves people to action.

Approach

From brand identities and packaging design to visual direction for music events, I approach every project as an opportunity to tell a compelling story. I work across diverse materials and formats, always grounded in research and driven by craft.
